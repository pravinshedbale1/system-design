# 📖 STORY BANK — Your Interview Weapon

> **For every system you design, you'll write a "2-Minute Interview Story" here.**
> 
> When an interviewer says "Design X," you don't start from zero.
> You start with a STORY — a narrative arc that shows you've thought deeply about the problem.
>
> Each story follows the same structure:
> 1. **The Hook** — "The interesting challenge about X is..."
> 2. **The Requirements** — What we need to support (users, scale, features)
> 3. **The Evolution** — Start simple → hit a wall → evolve
> 4. **The Key Insight** — The one architectural decision that makes it work
> 5. **The Tradeoff** — What we sacrificed and why it was worth it

---

## How to Use This Bank

Before an interview:
1. Skim the stories for the most likely systems
2. Practice narrating each one out loud (2 minutes max)
3. Focus on the KEY INSIGHT — that's what separates "memorized" from "understood"
