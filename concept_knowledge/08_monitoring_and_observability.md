## The Big Story

> _"Imagine driving a car with no dashboard — no speedometer, no fuel gauge, no warning lights.
> That's running a system without monitoring. The three pillars of observability — metrics, logs, 
> and traces — are your dashboard, black box, and GPS respectively."_

## The Three Pillars

| Pillar | Analogy | What It Tells You | Tool Examples |
|--------|---------|-------------------|---------------|
| **Metrics** | Car dashboard gauges | How fast, how hot, how full | Prometheus, Datadog, CloudWatch |
| **Logs** | Flight recorder / black box | What happened, step by step | ELK Stack, Splunk, CloudWatch Logs |
| **Traces** | GPS tracking a package | The journey of one request | Jaeger, Zipkin, Datadog APM |
